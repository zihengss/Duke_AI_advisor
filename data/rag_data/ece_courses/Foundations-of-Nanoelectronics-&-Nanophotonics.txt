Course Code: ECE 545
Course Name: Foundations of Nanoelectronics & Nanophotonics

Description:
Theory and applications of nanoelectronics and nanophotonics. Quantum dots and wells, metal nanoparticles, organic-inorganic interfaces, graphene, next generation transistors, light emitters, and sensors. Prerequisite: Electrical and Computer Engineering 230L and 270DL or equivalent.
